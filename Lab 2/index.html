                        An Introduction to #CyberFest:

 CyberFest was created to raise awareness of cyber security activities across the region as part of our mission
				  'To Make the world as the place for cyber security' 
				  and usually takes place as ten events across the region.
2022 marks the fifth year of #CyberFest and this year, we promise this festival will be bigger and better than ever. 
                              Our annual #CyberFest invites the cyber business community to 
			       come together, to connect, to debate, to explore contemporary
                  issues effecting businesses, to discover new perspectives and to listen and learn.
